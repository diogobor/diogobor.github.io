Profile

About & background

Holds a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and an M.Sc. in Computer Science with a focus on Artificial Intelligence from the Federal University of Rio de Janeiro (UFRJ).

Completed a Ph.D. in Biosciences and Biotechnology at the Carlos Chagas Institute / Fiocruz, developing the SIM-XL search tool for complex cross-linked peptide datasets in structural proteomics and protein–protein interaction studies.

Postdoctoral work at Institut Pasteur (Paris) focused on bioinformatics solutions for top-down proteomics, followed by research at Leibniz-FMP (Berlin) on cleavable cross-linking MS and fast peptide identification strategies. Currently a Research Scientist in computational proteomics at Exact Sciences.
