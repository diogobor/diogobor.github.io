Experience

  • Research Scientist - Exact Sciences, Munich - Germany (Since September/2024).

  • Post-doc in Computational Proteomics tailored toward Cross-linking Mass Spectrometry - The Liu Lab - Leibniz-Forschungsinstitut für Molekulare Pharmakologie (FMP), Berlin - Germany (2020-2024).
    Supervised by Dr. Fan Liu. We have developed Scout for identifying and quantifying cross-linked peptides using cleavable-reagents.

  • Data Scientist in Proteomics - Winter lab - CeMM, Vienna - Austria (Jan/2020 - Aug/2020).

  • Post-doc in Bioinformatics and Computational Biology - Mass Spectrometry for Biology Unit - Institut Pasteur - Paris, Paris - France (2016-2019). Supervised by Dr. Julia Chamot-Rooke. I've worked on an algorithm for top-down proteomics data analysis.

  • Ph.D. in Bioinformatics and Computational Biology - Carlos Chagas Institute - Fundação Oswaldo Cruz (Fiocruz), Curitiba - Brazil. (2013-2016) "Algorithm for identifying cross-linked peptides analyzed by mass spectrometry."
    I've developed with my supervisors, as Ph.D Thesis, a new search engine for identifying and annotating cross-linked peptides, called SIM-XL.
    Visualize our video demonstration to check how SIM-XL works!

  • M.Sc. in Computer Science and Systems Engineering - COPPE - Federal University of Rio de Janeiro - UFRJ, Rio de Janeiro - Brazil. (2011-2013) "Computational Method for identifying peptides labeled with phenylisothiocyanate analyzed by liquid chromatography coupled to tandem mass spectrometry" I have developed with Dr. Paulo C. Carvalho as Master's Thesis, a search tool mass spectrometry - SIM (Spectrum Identification Machine), using PSM approach.
